⚙️ Creator Programme
Tune platform-wide fee rates and creator programme settings. All rates are dynamic — changes take effect on the next trade or market resolution. No caps are enforced server-side; changes are your responsibility.
Programme Status
ACTIVE
When OFF: trade endpoint skips creator royalty accrual entirely. Proposal approvals and the suggestion reward continue to work — only trade-time royalty is paused.
Platform Fee Rates
Forecasting Fee (% of gross stake)
Deducted at trade time. Net amount enters the AMM pool. Default 5%.
Resolution Cut (% of net pool)
Platform share taken from the pool at resolution. Remainder distributed to winners. Default 20%.
Creator Programme
Royalty Rate (% of net trade)
Default 0.5%. Carved from the existing 5% forecasting fee — pool is unaffected.
Volume Threshold (KES)
Royalty activates once a market's totalVolume crosses this.
Suggestion Reward (KES)
One-time wallet credit when a market suggestion is approved.
Bounty Min Payout (KES)
Below this unpaid balance, manual bounty payout is disabled.
💬 WhatsApp Notifications
Control which transactional notification types are mirrored to WhatsApp. The Meta WhatsApp Business account is the same one used for OTP — keep templates clean to protect that critical login channel. Defaults to LOG-ONLY mode until WHATSAPP_NOTIFS_ENABLED env var is set to true in Vercel.
Global Switch
Emergency kill — disables ALL WhatsApp notifications regardless of per-type toggles. OTP is unaffected.
ON
Per-Type Toggles
Opt-out a user (STOP reply handler)
When a user replies STOP to a WhatsApp message, set them as opted-out here. They will continue to receive in-app notifications.
🎁 Referral Programme
Manage the refer-a-friend programme. When OFF, the Refer a Friend option, leaderboard badge, and reward logic all turn off simultaneously.
Programme Status
ACTIVE
Referrer Reward (KES)
Credited to referrer as real withdrawable balance
Referee Match Cap (KES)
Non-withdrawable bonus credited on first qualifying deposit
Minimum Deposit (KES)
Referee must reach this total deposit before bonus is credited
Minimum Trade Volume (KES)
Referee must reach this total trade volume before referrer is paid
⚠️ Scam risk: Minimum trade is below minimum deposit. A user could deposit, collect the bonus, then withdraw before trading. Set minimum trade ≥ minimum deposit.
