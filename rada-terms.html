Section 3

Nature of the Platform

CheckRada is an information-based forecasting platform, not a gambling or sports betting service. Forecasts are made on verifiable real-world outcomes including economic indicators, government policy decisions and public health targets. CheckRada does not operate under any sports betting licence.

Participation constitutes an incentivised data contribution, not a wager. The Platform generates crowd intelligence data as a commercial product independent of individual forecasting outcomes.

Section 5

Deposits and Withdrawals

All transactions are conducted in Kenyan Shillings (KES) via M-Pesa. The minimum deposit is KES 20. The minimum withdrawal is KES 50. Withdrawals are processed instantly to your registered M-Pesa number, subject to Safaricom M-Pesa service availability.

CheckRada reserves the right to request identity verification before processing withdrawals above certain thresholds as required by applicable Kenyan law including the Proceeds of Crime and Anti-Money Laundering Act.

Section 6

Forecasting and Fees

A 5% forecasting fee is applied to every position you take, regardless of outcome. This fee covers platform operations and market resolution costs.

Market creators earn 0.5% of every trade on their approved published market, credited automatically to their CheckRada wallet while the market is active.

Dispute Resolution Fee: In the event of a disputed Social Challenge outcome requiring Platform intervention to resolve as a tie, a total platform fee of 15% shall apply to the pool. This covers dispute resolution costs. The standard 5% fee applies to all other outcomes including mutual agreements reached before Platform intervention.
Section 7

Market Resolution

Public markets are resolved by the CheckRada Integrity Team based on official, publicly verifiable sources. In cases of conflicting reports, official government and regulatory data sources (e.g., KNBS, CBK, EPRA, Ministry publications) shall take precedence over media outlets. CheckRada's source selection and resolution decisions are final.

Social Challenges are resolved by a nominated Referee or by mutual agreement, with a 48-hour dispute window. If a market cannot be resolved due to postponement or ambiguity, all stakes are refunded in full with no fees deducted.
